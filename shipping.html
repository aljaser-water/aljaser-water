🚚 1. Shipping Destinations

We currently ship to all cities and regions across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA).

We do not currently ship outside Saudi Arabia. For international inquiries, please contact us on WhatsApp.

Cities we deliver to include:

  • Riyadh (الرياض) — 2–3 business days
  • Jeddah (جدة) — 2–3 business days
  • Dammam & Eastern Province (الدمام والمنطقة الشرقية) — 2–3 business days
  • Mecca (مكة المكرمة) — 2–4 business days
  • Medina (المدينة المنورة) — 2–4 business days
  • Khobar, Dhahran, Jubail — 2–3 business days
  • Abha, Taif, Tabuk, Hail and other regions — 3–5 business days

⏱️ 3. Processing & Delivery Times

Order Processing

Once you place your order via WhatsApp, we will confirm it within a few hours during working hours. Orders are processed and handed to the courier within 1–2 business days.

Orders placed on Friday, Saturday, or public holidays will be processed on the next working day (Sunday).

Delivery Times

After dispatch, expect delivery within:

  • Major cities (Riyadh, Jeddah, Dammam): 2–3 business days
  • Other regions: 3–5 business days
  • Remote areas: up to 7 business days

These are estimates and may vary during peak seasons (Ramadan, Eid holidays).

📍 4. Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, we will send you:

  • A WhatsApp message confirming your order has been shipped
  • The courier name and tracking number (when available)
  • An estimated delivery date

You can always check your order status by messaging us directly on WhatsApp at any time.

📦 5. Packaging

All orders are carefully packed to ensure your products arrive safely and in perfect condition.

  • Products are wrapped in protective padding
  • Glass bottles are individually wrapped to prevent breakage
  • Boxes are sealed and labeled with your delivery details

If your package arrives damaged, please take photos immediately and contact us on WhatsApp within 24 hours of receiving it.

⚠️ 6. Failed or Missed Deliveries

If the courier is unable to deliver your order:

  • They will attempt delivery a second time the following day
  • You will be contacted via phone or WhatsApp to arrange re-delivery
  • After 2 failed attempts, the order will be returned to us
If your order is returned due to a wrong address or unavailability, you may be charged an additional shipping fee for re-delivery.

Please ensure your delivery address and phone number are correct when placing your order.
