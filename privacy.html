📋 2. What Data We Collect

Under Article 5 of the Saudi E-Commerce Law Implementing Regulations, we collect only what is strictly necessary to process your order.

When you place an order:

  • Full name | الاسم الكامل
  • Delivery address in Saudi Arabia | عنوان التوصيل
  • Phone number for delivery coordination | رقم الجوال
  • Email address for order confirmation | البريد الإلكتروني
  • Payment info — encrypted and processed by Moyasar. We never see or store your card details.

When you browse:

  • Browser type and device type (site performance only)
  • Pages visited (to improve your experience)
  • We do NOT use advertising cookies or share browsing data with any third party

🎯 3. How We Use Your Data

Per Saudi PDPL requirements, we use your personal data only for:

  • ✅ Processing and fulfilling your order
  • ✅ Sending delivery updates and order confirmation
  • ✅ Responding to your questions and complaints
  • ✅ Issuing VAT-compliant invoices (required by ZATCA)
  • ✅ Improving our store and your shopping experience
❌ We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for marketing.
❌ We do NOT use your data for any other purpose without your explicit consent.

⏳ 4. How Long We Keep Your Data

We keep your data only as long as needed for the transaction and legal compliance:

  • Order records — up to 5 years (Saudi commercial record-keeping law)
  • Account data — until you request account deletion
  • Payment records — retained by Moyasar under SAMA regulations

You can request deletion of your data at any time. We will process your request within 30 days.

⚖️ 5. Your Rights — Saudi PDPL

As a consumer in Saudi Arabia, the Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL — effective September 2023) gives you the following rights:

  • Right to Know — Request info about what data we hold on you
  • Right to Access — Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Correction — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Deletion — Request erasure of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — Withdraw consent for data use at any time
  • Right to Complain — File a complaint with SDAIA at sdaia.gov.sa

🔒 6. Payment Security

  • Moyasar is PCI-DSS certified

  • All prices include 15% VAT as required by ZATCA (هيئة الزكاة والضريبة والجمارك)
