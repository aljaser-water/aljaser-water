🏪 Who You Are Buying From

You are purchasing from an independent authorized reseller of Aljaser Water products — not from شركة مصنع الجسر directly. All returns and refunds are handled by us, not by the manufacturer.

⚖️ Your Legal Right to Return

Under Article 13 of Saudi Arabia's E-Commerce Law (Royal Decree M/126, 2019), you have the right to return any product within 7 days of receiving your order — no questions asked — provided the conditions below are met.

✅ This right is guaranteed by Saudi law and cannot be waived or removed by any seller.
✅ Eligible for Return
  • Unopened, unused products
  • Original packaging intact
  • Within 7 days of receiving your order
  • Product arrived damaged or defective
  • Wrong item was sent
❌ Not Eligible for Return
  • Products that have been opened or used
  • Products with broken seals
  • More than 7 days since delivery
  • Products returned without original packaging

📦 How to Return a Product

Follow these steps to request a return:

  1. Contact us within 7 days of receiving your order — via WhatsApp or email (details below)
  2. Tell us your order number and the reason for return
  3. We will confirm your return request within 24 hours
  4. Return the product in its original, unopened condition. Return shipping costs are your responsibility unless the product was damaged or incorrect.
  5. Once we receive and inspect the product, your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days
📱 The fastest way to start a return is via WhatsApp. Send us your order number and a photo of the product.

💳 Refunds

Approved refunds will be returned to your original payment method:

  • MADA / Visa / Mastercard — refunded within 5–7 business days
  • STC Pay — refunded within 3–5 business days
  • Apple Pay — refunded within 5–7 business days

Refunds are processed through Moyasar (our payment gateway). The exact time depends on your bank.

⚠️ Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to a defective, damaged, or incorrect item sent by us.

📸 Damaged or Wrong Items

If your order arrives damaged or you received the wrong product:

  • Take a photo of the item immediately
  • Contact us via WhatsApp within 48 hours of delivery
  • We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you
  • Return shipping will be covered by us for damaged/wrong items

🚫 Order Cancellations

You may cancel your order under these conditions:

  • ✅ Before the order is shipped — full refund, no charge
  • ✅ After delivery — within the 7-day return window above
  • ❌ After 7 days from delivery — cancellation is not possible

To cancel before shipping, contact us as soon as possible via WhatsApp.

🏛️ Escalating a Complaint

If you are unsatisfied with our response, you have the right under Saudi Consumer Protection Law to escalate your complaint:

  • 📱 Ministry of Commerce consumer complaints: 1900 (toll-free)
  • 🌐 Online: mc.gov.sa
  • 🌐 Consumer Protection Association: cpa.org.sa
